Fajitas

Description

This simple and tasty dish will fill the bellies of your family with healthy nutritious food. Easy to cook with cheap ingredients, it'll soon be a family favourite

Ingredients

  • Minced Beef
  • Vegetables
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Spices
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Fry mince until browned.
  2. Slice vegetables and fry, then add to mince
  3. Add tomatoes and spices and stir
  4. Serve with rice
