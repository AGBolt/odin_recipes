Fajitas

Description

This simple and tasty dish will fill the bellies of your family with healthy nutritious food. Easy to cook with cheap ingredients, it'll soon be a family favourite

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Vegetables
  • Wraps
  • Spice mix
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into pieces and fry until browned.
  2. Slice vegetables and fry, then add to chicken
  3. Add spice mix and stir
  4. Serve with wraps and salsa
