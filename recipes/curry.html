Curry

Description

This chicken curry is deliciousand very simple to make, using ingredients you have in your cupboard. Perfect as a weekday meal for a healthy alternative for all the family

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Spices
  • Rice
  • love

Steps

  1. Fry vegetables in oil, add spices
  2. Cut chicken up and fry
  3. cook rice
  4. Serve while hot, and sprinkle with coriander
